Credits

PyroCMS was originally written by Phil Sturgeon under the name StyleCMS as a simple way to build CodeIgniter based brochureware and webapps that would normally need the same core features with a few extra modules. After his company went down in flames in the recession, StyleCMS was renamed to PyroCMS and released open-source in 2009.

Since then we have had a huge number of contributions from the community and formed the PyroCMS Development Team:
